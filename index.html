What is Improv Theater?

“Improvisation Theater is the form of theatre, often comedy, in which most or all of what is performed is unplanned or unscripted, created spontaneously by the performer.”

Source: Wikipedia — Creative Commons

Quick Overview

Improvisational theatre (or improv) emphasizes collaboration, listening, and playful risk-taking.

This site is intended to give you an introduction to types of improv, suggest places to see improv, and maybe even inspire you to try improv yourself!
