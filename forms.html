Forms of Improv

There are two main types of improv

Long Form: improv performers create shows or sets in which short scenes are often interrelated by story, characters, or themes.

Short Form: improv that consists of short scenes usually constructed from a predetermined game, structure, or idea and driven by an audience suggestion.
